Everything you need to capture your screen

Powerful where it counts, private by default.

High-quality capture

Windows Graphics Capture records any monitor or window at up to 1080p / 60 fps, with GPU frames kept on the GPU.

System & mic audio

Mix system (WASAPI loopback) audio and your microphone into a single, in-sync track.

Hardware acceleration

Encodes H.264/HEVC on NVIDIA NVENC, Intel Quick Sync, or AMD AMF — with a Windows Media Foundation software fallback.

Upload-ready MP4

Faststart MP4 output that's ready to post to YouTube and anywhere else — no re-encoding step.

Private by design

Runs entirely offline. No account, no analytics, no telemetry — your recordings never leave your device.

Yours to control

Pick resolution, frame rate, encoder, and output folder. Pause and resume a recording whenever you like.
